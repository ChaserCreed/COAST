COAST

Cheap, Open-source American Sign Translator

COAST is a non-profit project designed as a wristband, aiming to bridge the gap between the hearing and non-hearing communities. It uses energy-efficient acoustic wave technology to track hand shapes, sensors to capture motion, and AI-powered software to transcribe ASL into written language in real time via Bluetooth.

This initiative is focused on providing an accessible, affordable, and open-source solution for live ASL translation, empowering individuals with an inclusive tool that fosters communication and connection.
