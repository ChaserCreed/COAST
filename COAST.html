COAST: Breaking Language Barriers

Increasing the capabilities of AI-powered ASL translation with open-source technology.

Our Mission

COAST is a non-profit wristband project dedicated to bridging the gap between hearing and non-hearing individuals using hand-tracking sensors and AI.

How It Works

Utilizing energy-efficient acoustic wave technology based on the EchoWrist project from researchers at Cornell University, COAST tracks hand movements and converts ASL into written language via Bluetooth.

Open-Source Commitment

All hardware and software are published on GitHub, making it accessible for anyone to build and improve.
